GoStar Digital LLC ("we", "our", or "us") operates the GoTrotters cognitive training platform. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our platform.

GoTrotters is designed with privacy in mind. We collect only what's necessary to deliver a great cognitive training experience.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect information that you or your facility provides directly to us:

  • Account Information: Username, 4-digit PIN, facility association
  • Training Data: Game scores, tier progression, session times, personal bests
  • Usage Data: Features used, language settings
  • Facility Data: Facility name, contact information, license details

HOW WE USE INFORMATION

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our cognitive training platform
  • Track GoTrotter progress through our 10-tier Championship system
  • Generate progress reports for facilities
  • Personalize the training experience
  • Communicate about account and service updates
  • Process payments and manage facility licenses

DATA SHARING

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Licensed Facilities: Facilities that manage GoTrotter accounts can view training progress and activity
  • Service Providers: Third parties that help operate our platform (hosting, payment processing via Stripe)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

DATA SECURITY

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Secure HTTPS connections
  • Encrypted data storage
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security reviews

DATA RETENTION

We retain GoTrotter training data for the duration of the facility's active license. After a license expires:

  • Trial data is preserved for 30 days
  • Paid license data is retained for 90 days after expiration
  • Data can be exported upon request before deletion

YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal and training data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

GoTrotters is designed primarily for adult cognitive wellness. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify facilities of significant changes via email and update the "Last updated" date above.
